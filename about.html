JoetechAI was founded with a simple mission — to make powerful software accessible to every business. We combine modern technology with intuitive design to help organizations operate smarter, faster, and more efficiently.

Our Story

From a Simple Idea to a Powerful Platform

JoetechAI started in 2020 when our founders recognized a gap in the market — businesses needed affordable, easy-to-use software that could adapt to their unique needs without requiring extensive technical expertise.

What began as a small project to build a simple store management tool quickly evolved into a comprehensive software platform serving businesses across multiple industries. Today, JoetechAI powers operations for over 50 businesses and 10,000 users worldwide.

Our team has grown from 2 founders to 30+ dedicated professionals, all united by a shared vision of making enterprise-grade software accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

Key milestones in our growth and evolution.

First 10 Customers
